Behind the Lens

Hi, I'm Kim! Thanks for stopping by. This is me and my two kiddos, Brandon and Ryan.

I'm using the Web Design courses at WCC to introduce my photography for the first time. Although I grew up with a professional photographer (my Dad!), I didn't pursue photography until a friend asked for a favor. "Use your Dad's camera and take photos at my wedding, please?" I LOVED the experience and soon after, I invested in my own camera.

Although I generally depise cold weather, I discovered that I LOVE to take portraits in the snow. The crisp colors of warm hats, sweaters and rosy cheeks against the clean snow makes a beautiful image. And my second favorite? Spring! Colorful blooms on the trees make a gorgeous backdrop. Summer is my favorite season, and the extended daylight hours mean beautiful lighting. And the brilliant colors of fall are hard to resist. So...this proves that ANY season is a good season to take photos, and to capture lifelong memories.

I am a life-long mid-westerner, originally from Central Illinois and moved to the western suburbs after college. I love to TRAVEL and would gladly take photos at a destination wedding, especially one where the weather is warm and there are plenty of palm trees [hint, hint]. I also love COUNTRY MUSIC (...and have been known to do the boot scootin' boogie at wedding receptions), GOLF (still trying to break 100), and LATTES (caramel is the best)!
